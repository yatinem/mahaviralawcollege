LORD MAHAVIRA COLLEGE OF LAW, BIJNA KASHIPUR ROAD, MORADABAD was established in the year 2015. The aim of the College Society is to fulfill its cherished ambition to establish an institution for imparting legal education. The law college has qualified full time principal and faculty members. It should be a pleasure to learn your course at the hands of experienced teachers. In addition experienced advocates are appointed to teach procedural laws. The college is relentlessly striving in the pursuance of academic excellence and encourages the students to participate spontaneously in various co-curricular and extra-curricular activities. This conducive ambience fosters creativity, intensity, fortitude and leadership qualities among students for development of versatile personality.
